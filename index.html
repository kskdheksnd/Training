Training Techniques

Ball Control & Dribbling

Players perform cone drills, tight-space dribbling, and ball-touch repetitions to improve close control.

Passing & Playmaking

Training includes short and long passing routines, tactical patterns, and teamwork-based drills.

Shooting & Finishing

Strikers and midfielders practice accuracy, power shots, volleys, and situational finishing.
